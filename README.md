# User Stories
### User Login
Users can log into their account to view and manage orders.     Priority: 10    Effort: 2 days
### Register Account
New users can create an account to order food.                  Priority: 10    Effort: 2 days
### Browse Menu
Students can view food menus and choose food items.             Priority: 10    Effort: 3 days
### Shopping Cart
Users can add multiple food items before placing an order.      Priority: 20    Effort: 3 days
### Order Tracking
Users can check the progress of their food order.               Priority: 20    Effort: 2 days
### Menu Management
Staff can add, edit, or remove food items from the menu.        Priority: 20    Effort: 3 days
### Payment Option
Users can pay online for faster checkout.                       Priority: 30    Effort: 4 days
### Notifications
Users receive updates when their food is ready.                 Priority: 30    Effort: 2 days
### Profile Settings
Users can update their account details.                         Priority: 40    Effort: 1 day
### Dark Mode
Users can switch to dark mode for easier viewing at night.      Priority: 50    Effort: 1 day
